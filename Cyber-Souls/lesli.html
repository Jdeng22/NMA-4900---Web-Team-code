Lesli Cuamani : Pursuit of Happyness

Pursuit of Happiness utilizes photos, videos, and a website to share personal moments that represent happiness during troubling times.

Click on the Image

Project Statement:

When the pandemic started, it drastically changed my life and routines. I went from commuting to school almost everyday, working at an office with colleagues, participating in on-campus activities and so much more to staying at home for the next 8 or so months. I felt lonesome, like I was missing out on so many opportunities and experiences, and most importantly, that I wouldn’t be able to go back to what I thought was my “normal” life. I would take pictures and randomly record videos in moments when I felt like things were okay and I felt genuine happiness. Therefore, as the optimist that I am, I decided to reflect and reevaluate a lot of the experiences I went through before the pandemic in college, during the pandemic, and what my life looks like in the present. By reminding myself of the little things in life that bring me joy, I hope to continue seeking that sensation of happiness in what I do. Whether it be spending time with my dog, seeing my baby brother grow, eating out with my friends, or traveling to places, I continue to take into consideration the things that have transformed me and pushed me to grow in various ways due to the pandemic. Pursuit of Happyness is a compilation of memories and moments that share just that: happiness discovered during times of uncertainty.

Artist Statement:

Lesli Cuamani is a New York born and Queens raised multi-media artist whose primary work takes form as web development, photography, and video. As a first generation Mexican-American, she is heavily influenced by her Mexican culture and values. Her new media artwork is about her personal identity and her deep interest in appreciating the present. “Tomorrow is never promised so live today like it’s your last.” Lesli enjoys capturing photos and videos of her family, her dogs, her friends, her travels, and her food. Lesli hopes to share positive feelings and create positive experiences through her work, inspiring others to take action and be in pursuit of their own happiness.
